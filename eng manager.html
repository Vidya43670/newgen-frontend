Engineering Manager Roadmap

🔧 What Is This Role?

Engineering Managers lead software engineering teams, focusing on delivering high-quality products while mentoring developers, managing projects, and aligning with business goals.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Solid engineering background, systems design, agile practices Leetcode, System Design Primer, Scrum
🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Team People management, 1:1s, performance reviews, hiring 15Five, Lattice, Greenhouse
🛠 Strategy Roadmapping, tech debt management, scaling teams Jira, Notion, Linear, Confluence
🚀 Lead Cross-functional collaboration, stakeholder alignment, mentoring OKRs, Tech talks, Retrospectives

🧠 Skills You’ll Need

  • Strong technical foundation (coding, architecture)
  • Leadership & communication
  • Project & people management
  • Strategic thinking

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • Certified Scrum Master
  • Project Management Professional (PMP)
  • Engineering Management Institute Training
