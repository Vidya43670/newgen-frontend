Business Consultant Roadmap

🔧 What Is This Role?

Business Consultants analyze business problems, develop strategies, and advise companies on how to improve performance, profitability, and efficiency. They bridge technical, financial, and management perspectives.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Learn business fundamentals, market research, and analytical thinking Harvard Business School Online, Business Model Canvas
⚙️ Tools Develop skills in Excel, PowerPoint, SWOT, and financial modeling Google Sheets, Tableau, MS Excel, Lucidchart
🧠 Build Work on real-world case studies, client interaction, and stakeholder management Consulting simulations, mock projects, management tools
🚀 Advance Lead strategic initiatives, specialize in an industry, or pursue MBA McKinsey Case Studies, BCG, Bain resources
