UX Researcher Roadmap

🔍 What Is This Role?

UX Researchers help teams understand user behaviors, needs, and motivations through observation techniques and feedback collection. Their insights guide product design and functionality.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Learn UX principles, human-centered design, and research ethics UX Crash Course – YouTube
🔬 Research Skills Practice usability testing, surveys, interviews, A/B testing Google Forms, Maze, Optimal Workshop
🧠 Data Handling Analyze user behavior and qualitative/quantitative data Excel, Dovetail, Hotjar, Google Analytics
🚀 Advanced Work on case studies, collaborate with design teams Figma, Notion, Airtable

📚 Learn from These

  • UX Research – Coursera
  • Nielsen Norman Group UX Research Course
  • UX Design Process – YouTube

🎯 Skills You’ll Need

  • User-centered thinking
  • Research planning and analysis
  • Communication & reporting
  • Empathy and attention to detail

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • Google UX Design Certificate
  • Nielsen Norman UX Research Certification
  • Interaction Design Foundation (IDF) Courses
