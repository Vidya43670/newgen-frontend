Learning Metrics Specialist Roadmap

📘 What Is This Role?

A Learning Metrics Specialist focuses on measuring and evaluating the effectiveness of learning experiences, programs, and policies. They use data to assess learner outcomes, engagement, and progress to inform decisions in educational institutions and EdTech platforms.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Examples / Topics
📘 Foundation Understand basics of education systems, assessment strategies, and learning models Bloom’s Taxonomy, Learning Objectives, Basic Statistics
📚 Analytics & Data Develop skills in learning analytics, data visualization, and impact measurement LMS Data, Dashboards, Learning Record Stores
🧠 Advanced Tools Use advanced tools and methodologies to evaluate learner progress and performance Power BI, Python, R, Tableau, SPSS
🏫 Implementation Collaborate with educators and stakeholders to improve curriculum through evidence-based insights Data-Driven Feedback, Continuous Improvement Models
