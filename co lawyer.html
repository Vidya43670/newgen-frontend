Corporate Lawyer Roadmap

🔧 What Is This Role?

Corporate Lawyers advise companies on their legal obligations, contracts, mergers and acquisitions, and corporate governance. They work to ensure businesses comply with laws and minimize legal risks.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Earn a law degree (LLB or JD), understand constitutional, contract and business law Harvard Online Law School, NLU India
⚙️ Internships Intern under corporate law firms and gain exposure to real-world business issues AZB Partners, Khaitan & Co., Trilegal
🧠 Specialize Study mergers and acquisitions, securities law, arbitration, and compliance Bar Council of India guidelines, SEBI regulations
🚀 Advance Handle corporate transactions, advise on IPOs, negotiate international deals Deloitte, PwC Legal, Ernst & Young

📚 Learn from These

  • Corporate Law – HarvardX
  • Business Law – Coursera
  • Institute of Company Secretaries of India

🧠 Skills You’ll Need

  • Strong legal research and writing
  • Understanding of business operations
  • Contract negotiation & drafting
  • Risk analysis and compliance management

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • LL.M. in Corporate Law
  • Diploma in Business Law (NALSAR, ILI, etc.)
