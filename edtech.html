EdTech Creator Roadmap

🎓 What Is This Role?

An EdTech Creator designs and develops innovative educational technologies and content that enhance learning experiences. This role blends technology, pedagogy, and creativity to build tools, courses, and resources for students, teachers, and institutions.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Examples / Topics
🎓 Foundation Understand learning theories, instructional design, and digital literacy Bloom's Taxonomy, ADDIE Model, Learning Styles
🛠️ Tools Learn tools for content creation and LMS platforms Articulate, Canva, Moodle, Adobe Captivate
🚀 Build Design and deploy interactive courses or learning apps Gamification, Adaptive Learning, E-learning Modules
🧠 Innovate Experiment with AI, AR/VR, and data-driven learning platforms AI Tutors, Analytics Dashboards, Virtual Labs
