Academic Counsellor Roadmap

📘 What Is This Role?

An Academic Counsellor helps students understand their educational options, make academic plans, and overcome obstacles that may hinder academic success. They play a key role in student retention, development, and career readiness.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Examples / Topics
🎓 Foundation Earn a degree in education, psychology, or counseling B.Ed, BA/BSc in Psychology, MSW
📚 Core Skills Develop knowledge in academic advising, mental health basics, and communication Student Counseling, Academic Planning, Communication Skills
🔍 Practice Gain experience in school/university environments, intern with counselors Peer Mentoring, School Counseling Internships
📈 Advance Pursue specialization, join professional associations, attend training Career Counseling Certification, NACADA Membership
