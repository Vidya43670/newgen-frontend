AR/VR Artist Roadmap

🎨 What Is This Role?

An AR/VR Artist designs immersive and interactive digital experiences using technologies like augmented reality (AR) and virtual reality (VR). They blend creativity with technology to create stunning 3D environments and characters for gaming, education, marketing, and more.

🚀 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Examples / Topics
🎓 Foundation Learn fundamentals of design, 3D modeling, and digital art Photoshop, Blender, Maya, Design Principles
🔧 Build Skills Explore game engines and immersive tech tools Unity, Unreal Engine, Vuforia, ARKit
📐 Portfolio Create interactive demos and environments 3D scenes, character designs, motion interactions
🌍 Advance Work on industry projects, contribute to AR/VR innovations AR filters, VR storytelling, UX in immersive spaces
