Innovation Designer Roadmap

🚀 What Is This Role?

An Innovation Designer blends design thinking with strategic foresight to build breakthrough products, services, and solutions. This role focuses on user needs, creative ideation, and implementing innovation in business or social contexts.

🛤️ Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Examples / Topics
🌱 Foundation Basics of design thinking, creativity, and visual storytelling Empathy maps, ideation techniques, storyboarding
📚 Skill Building Master human-centered design, research and prototyping User journeys, design sprints, service blueprints
🔬 Practice Work on real-world innovation projects or internships OpenIDEO challenges, hackathons, social innovation
🎯 Specialize Develop strategy, systems thinking, and impact design Futures thinking, ecosystem mapping, policy design

  • Master’s in Innovation or Strategic Design
  • Design Leadership Programs (e.g. Rotman, Hyper Island)
