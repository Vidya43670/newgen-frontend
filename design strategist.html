Design Strategist Roadmap

🎯 What Is This Role?

A Design Strategist bridges the gap between design thinking and business goals. They identify user needs, align them with business objectives, and create strategies for innovative solutions. This role involves research, facilitation, and cross-functional collaboration.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Examples / Topics
🔍 Understand Basics of design thinking, user research, and business models Empathy Mapping, Business Model Canvas
🧠 Strategy Apply strategic thinking to product or service development Value Proposition, Customer Journey Mapping
📊 Execute Design facilitation, sprint planning, stakeholder alignment Workshops, Design Sprints, Strategy Decks
🚀 Advance Lead innovation efforts, coach design teams, publish insights Thought Leadership, Conference Talks, Case Studies
