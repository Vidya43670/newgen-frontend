Creative Manager

💡 What Is This Role?

A Creative Manager or Art Director leads the visual direction of projects in advertising, branding, and media. They combine design, storytelling, and team leadership to bring ideas to life.

🚀 Career Roadmap

Stage Focus Area Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Learn design basics: color, layout, typography Canva Color Wheel
🎯 Intermediate Master design tools & branding concepts Photoshop, Illustrator, Figma
🧑‍💼 Management Skills Team coordination, client communication, project leadership LinkedIn Learning
🧠 Portfolio Phase Build a strong design portfolio across formats Behance, Dribbble, Adobe Portfolio
