Epidemiologist Roadmap

🦠 What Is This Role?

Epidemiologists study disease outbreaks, track patterns, and analyze data to help prevent public health crises. They work with governments, research institutions, and healthcare providers.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Biostatistics, public health basics, epidemiologic methods CDC Courses, Coursera Public Health
🧪 Tools Data analysis, spreadsheets, and statistical software R, SAS, SPSS, Excel
📊 Data Work Analyze disease trends, interpret reports, build visualizations Tableau, Power BI, ArcGIS
🔬 Advance Specialize in infectious disease, chronic illness, or global health WHO Training, MPH or PhD

📚 Learn from These

  • Epidemiology Specialization – Coursera
  • CDC Epidemiology Training
  • Epidemics – FutureLearn

🔧 Skills You’ll Need

  • Statistical analysis
  • Critical thinking
  • Medical and biological understanding
  • Data visualization and reporting

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • Certified in Public Health (CPH)
  • Applied Epidemiology Certificate – CDC
  • Masters of Public Health (MPH)
