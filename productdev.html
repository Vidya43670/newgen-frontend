Product Developer Roadmap

🔧 What Is This Role?

Product Developers are responsible for turning ideas into tangible products. They work on product design, prototyping, testing, and aligning development with user needs and market trends.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Understand product lifecycle, market research, and user needs Design Thinking, Lean Startup, Competitive Analysis
⚙️ Tools Explore tools for prototyping and user feedback Figma, Adobe XD, Balsamiq, UsabilityHub
🧠 Build Create MVPs, test concepts, and gather feedback iteratively Rapid Prototyping, User Interviews, Surveys
🚀 Advance Collaborate with product managers, marketing, and developers Agile, Jira, A/B Testing, Roadmapping
