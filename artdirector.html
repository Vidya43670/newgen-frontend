Art Director

💡 What Is This Role?

An Art Director leads the visual design direction for creative projects—ensuring consistency across branding, marketing, advertising, and user experience while guiding a team of designers and creatives.

🚀 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Learn graphic design basics, color theory, typography Canva, Adobe Illustrator, Figma
🎯 Intermediate Practice branding, layout design, team collaboration How to Art Direct - Adobe Creative Cloud
🧑‍🎨 Portfolio Phase Create mock campaigns and case studies for clients Behance, Adobe Express, Dribbble
🧠 Advanced Lead creative strategy, manage creative teams Creative Cloud, InVision, Miro, Trello
