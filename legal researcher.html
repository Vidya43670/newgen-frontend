Legal Researcher Roadmap

🔧 What Is This Role?

A Legal Researcher investigates case laws, statutes, regulations, and academic materials to provide insights and documentation for legal proceedings, policy work, or academic research.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Understand legal systems, case reading, and legal writing basics Indian Kanoon, LegalEdge, NALSAR notes
🧰 Skills Learn legal research methodology, citation practices Bluebook, Manupatra, SCC Online
🔍 Explore Research across domains like constitutional, IP, or environmental law LexisNexis, JSTOR, HeinOnline
🏛️ Apply Assist professors, law firms, or policy think tanks with research work NLUD Journals, Vidhi Centre, PRSIndia.org

📚 Learn from These

  • Legal Research for Beginners – Coursera
  • Indian Law Institute
  • Legal Writing Academy

🧠 Skills You’ll Need

  • Legal reading & comprehension
  • Legal writing & analysis
  • Citation formats (Bluebook, ILI)
  • Research tools & legal databases

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • Certificate in Legal Research Methodology – ILI
  • Advanced Diploma in Legislative Drafting – IGNOU
  • Certified Legal Researcher – NALSAR/LegalEdge
