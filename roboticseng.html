Robotics Engineer Roadmap

🔧 What Is This Role?

Robotics Engineers design, build, and maintain robots and robotic systems. They integrate mechanical, electrical, and software engineering to automate tasks and solve real-world problems.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Learn physics, calculus, basic electronics, and programming Python, C++, Arduino, Raspberry Pi
⚙️ Core Skills Understand mechanics, sensors, microcontrollers, and control theory ROS, MATLAB, SolidWorks, Simulink
🧠 Build Develop projects with robotic arms, autonomous vehicles, or drones Gazebo, OpenCV, LIDAR, SLAM
🚀 Advance Integrate AI, computer vision, and machine learning into robotics TensorFlow, PyTorch, ROS 2

📚 Learn from These

  • ColumbiaX Robotics Program – edX
  • Robotics Specialization – Coursera
  • Robotics: Learn by Building – Udemy

🧠 Skills You’ll Need

  • Embedded systems & sensors
  • Control systems & actuators
  • Robot Operating System (ROS)
  • Computer vision & perception

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • Certified Robotics Programmer (Fanuc, KUKA, etc.)
  • ROS Developer Certificate – The Construct
  • National Instruments Certification
