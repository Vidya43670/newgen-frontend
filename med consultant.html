Medical Consultant Roadmap

🩺 What Is This Role?

A Medical Consultant provides expert medical advice to healthcare organizations, pharmaceutical companies, or legal teams. They analyze data, guide decisions, and ensure medical accuracy in clinical operations and documentation.

🗺 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
🎓 Education Earn an MBBS or equivalent medical degree Medical colleges, USMLE/NEET PG
📖 Specialization Choose a clinical specialization or management stream (e.g., Internal Medicine, Hospital Admin) DNB, MD, MBA Healthcare Management
🧠 Experience Work in hospitals, research, or health companies Clinical practice, Case review, Advisory panels
💼 Transition Join as a consultant for pharma, tech, or policy orgs Pfizer, WHO, McKinsey Health, Practo
