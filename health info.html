Health Informatics Specialist

🩺 What Is This Role?

Health Informatics Specialists analyze healthcare data and use information systems to improve patient care, clinical workflows, and hospital management.

📈 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Understand healthcare systems, medical terminology, and basic IT WHO standards, HIPAA, ICD-10, HL7
🧠 Intermediate Learn EHRs, interoperability, and healthcare data standards Epic, Cerner, FHIR, SNOMED CT
📊 Analytics Analyze patient data and hospital metrics Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau
🚀 Advanced Explore AI in health, predictive analytics, and policy impact Python, R, Healthcare NLP, Kaggle Datasets
