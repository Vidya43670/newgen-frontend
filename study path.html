Explore Study Pathways

After 10th

Intermediate - MPC

Subjects: Maths, Physics, Chemistry
Who can opt: Students interested in Engineering, Defence, Architecture, etc.
Future Options: B.Tech, B.Sc., NDA, CA Foundation (optional).

Intermediate - BiPC

Subjects: Biology, Physics, Chemistry
Who can opt: Those interested in Medicine, Pharmacy, Agriculture, etc.
Future Options: MBBS, BDS, BPT, BHMS, B.Pharm.

Intermediate - MEC

Subjects: Mathematics, Economics, Commerce
Who can opt: Students interested in commerce, economics, business, or finance domains
Future Options: B.Com, BBA, CA, CMA, CS, Economics (Hons), Law

Intermediate - CEC

Subjects: Civics, Economics, Commerce
Who can opt: Those interested in commerce, law, public administration
Future Options: B.Com, BA, LLB, BBA.

Intermediate - HEC

Subjects: History, Economics, Civics
Who can opt: Students interested in arts, civil services, and humanities
Future Options: BA, Journalism, Law, UPSC.

Diploma

Fields: Engineering, Design, Animation, etc.
Who can opt: Students wanting early practical education
Future Options: Polytechnic, Lateral entry into Engineering.

ITI

Fields: Electrician, Fitter, Welding, etc.
Who can opt: Those aiming for industrial/technical jobs
Future Options: Apprenticeships, government/railway jobs.
After 12th

Engineering

Popular Streams: CSE, ECE, Civil, Mechanical
Entry: Through JEE, EAMCET, etc.
Careers: Software, Core Engineering, Public Sector.

Medical

Courses: MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS
Entry: Through NEET
Careers: Doctor, Surgeon, Health Professional.

Commerce

Courses: B.Com, CA, CS, CMA
Who can opt: Students with interest in finance and business
Careers: Accountant, Auditor, Analyst.

Arts/Humanities

Courses: BA, BSW, Journalism
Careers: Writer, Civil Services, Psychologist.

After Graduation

Post Graduation (M.Tech, M.Sc, MBA, etc.)

Streams: Technical, Science, Management
Purpose: Deep specialization or management shift
Exams: GATE, CAT, etc.

Government Exams

Popular: UPSC, SSC, Banking, Railways
Fields: Admin services, Defense, Finance
Prep Time: 1–2 years typically

Abroad Studies

Exams: GRE, TOEFL, IELTS
Courses: MS, MBA, Research
Countries: USA, UK, Canada, Germany

Skill-based Programs

Types: Coding bootcamps, design schools, digital marketing
Outcome: Direct job placements
