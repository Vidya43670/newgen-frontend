Medical Researcher Roadmap

🔬 What Is This Role?

Medical Researchers study diseases, treatments, and health data to improve healthcare outcomes. They design and conduct experiments, analyze data, and publish findings that impact clinical practices and public health.

🧭 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Basics of biology, chemistry, and statistics CrashCourse, Khan Academy
🔬 Training Learn research methodology, ethics, and lab skills Intro to Clinical Research – YouTube
📝 Research Phase Conduct research, analyze data, publish papers SPSS, R, PubMed, JAMA
🎓 Specialize Focus on oncology, neurology, epidemiology, etc. NIH, WHO, ResearchGate

📚 Learn from These

  • Clinical Research – Coursera
  • Biomedical Research – edX
  • YouTube – Research Basics

🧠 Skills You’ll Need

  • Scientific method & lab safety
  • Data collection & analysis (Excel, R, SPSS)
  • Ethical research practices
  • Academic writing and publishing

🎓 Certifications (Optional)

  • GCP (Good Clinical Practice)
  • NIH Clinical Research Training
  • CITI Program Certificate
