Product Designer Roadmap

🎨 What Is This Role?

Product Designers create intuitive, user-centered products by combining UX/UI design, interaction design, and strategy. They ensure designs meet both user needs and business goals.

🛤 Career Roadmap

Stage What to Focus On Tools / Examples
📘 Foundation Learn design thinking, color theory, typography UX Crash Course – DesignCourse
🛠 Tools Master design and prototyping tools Figma, Adobe XD, Sketch, InVision
📐 Build Design complete user flows and responsive UIs Portfolio projects, Dribbble, Behance
🚀 Advance UX strategy, user testing, and design systems Maze, Hotjar, Google UX Playbook
